## Description

This is the second game from the same notebook. It is a tamagotchi-like pet simulator where you grow Andrew to age 20.

- Press `С/П` to see Andrew. Press again and the program starts.
- The growth value increases every five successful life cycles.
- Goal: reach age 20 without Andrew dying.

Controls:

1. Feed — `BP 65` + `С/П`.
2. Walk — `BP 84` + `С/П`.
3. Sleep — `BP 75` + `С/П`, then press `С/П` again to wake.
4. Doctor visit (at age 10) — `BP 55` + `С/П`.

The game ends with `8 ЕС` on success and `8-----8` on failure.
