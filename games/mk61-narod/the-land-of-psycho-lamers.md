# The Land of Psycho Lamers

- Author: illi4 (as listed on the original page)
- Source: [mk61.narod.ru](https://mk61.narod.ru/game2.htm)
- Program listing: Not available in the local snapshot of `tmp/game2.htm`

## Description

You are an adventurer.
You need to find parts to assemble a computer.
You can discover the map, pick up items, and try to defeat the "lamers" (hostile
characters).

For movement, the page explains the following sequence:

1. Press `BP`.
2. Enter the target map cell number (as shown on the map).
3. Press `C/P`.

You can also see a list of places and items on the game board.
