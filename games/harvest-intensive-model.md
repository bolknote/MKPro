# Harvest Intensive Model

- Author: illi4
- Source: [Habr article](https://habr.com/ru/articles/125484/)
- Program: [harvest-intensive-model.txt](harvest-intensive-model.txt)

## Description

Management game focused on raising productivity on existing farmland.

## Gameplay

You manage a state-owned farm and must:

1. Allocate land and labour between crops.
2. Collect mushrooms and berries.
3. Invest in protection and operational costs.
4. Pay mandatory state contributions.

## Note

This is the intensive model variant, where yield growth is mainly driven by productivity improvements.
