- Author: illi4
- Source: [Habr article](https://habr.com/ru/articles/125484/)
- Program: [lunolet-d.txt](lunolet-d.txt)

## Description

A dynamic Lunolet variant where thrust switch and gravity constants are controlled by the calculator’s `R/G` mode.

## Gameplay

- `R` position multiplies gravity by 4 for the burn mode.
- `ГРД` balances gravity.
- `Г` switches the engine off.

On landing or crash, speed appears in `X`; remaining fuel appears in `Y`.
