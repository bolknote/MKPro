# Kon-Tiki: Место под Солнцем

- Source: [1985-10-put](https://web.archive.org/web/20110924184544id_/http://epizodsspace.no-ip.org/bibl/tm/1985/10/put.html)
- Program: [kon-tiki-mesto-pod-solntsem.txt](kon-tiki-mesto-pod-solntsem.txt)
- Series: Kon-Tiki / Путь к Земле

## Notes

Computes maximum apparent angular separation from the Sun for planets as seen from another planet. The magazine used the B3-34 glyph ↑ in indirect-register commands; this listing records the same opcode as register E.

The listing was restored from the archived text of the magazine page and kept as a byte-level program listing rather than matched by description.
