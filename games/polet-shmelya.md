# Полет шмеля

- Source: *Техника - молодежи*, No. 3, 1988, «Клуб электронных игр», article «Полет шмеля»
- Program: [polet-shmelya.txt](polet-shmelya.txt)
- Listing restored from DjVu scan with macOS Vision OCR and manual table check.

The game models a bumblebee flying through a 7x9 m house. The bumblebee enters through the facade door, must visit the rooms and corridors, then leave through a window.

Flight is controlled with the calculator mode switch positions `Р-ГРД-Г`: the display alternates between the bumblebee position and the number of maneuvers already made.
