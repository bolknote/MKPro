# Apollo 11 Launch from Earth (Atmosfera-1 KEI)

- Source: [Arbinada — Аполлон 11. Старт с Земли (МК61)](https://pmk.arbinada.com/category/01617.html)
- Program: [apollo11-launch-kei.txt](apollo11-launch-kei.txt)

## Description
Early MK-61 variant used for Earth's launch phase to orbit, based on the classic KEI Atmosphere-1 implementation.

It simulates early ascent with simple atmospheric model and includes manual staging workflow and flight-control loop logic for launch conditions.
