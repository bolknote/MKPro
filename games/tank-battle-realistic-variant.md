# Tank Battle (Realistic Variant)

- Author: illi4
- Source: [Habr article](https://habr.com/ru/articles/125484/)
- Additional source: [xbasoft PMK package](https://xvadim.github.io/xbasoft/pmk/tanksbattler.zip)
- Program: [tank-battle-realistic-variant.txt](tank-battle-realistic-variant.txt)

## Description

The realistic variant of the tank battle where the player does not know the weather coefficient before the shot.

## Notes

This makes engagement outcomes less predictable and closer to a live duel setup.

The xbasoft `tanksbattler` PMK listing is byte-identical to this file, so the duplicate local variant is not kept separately.
