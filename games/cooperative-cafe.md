# Cooperative Cafe

- Source: *Техника - молодежи*, No. 5, 1988, «Клуб электронных игр», article «Кооперативное кафе»
- Transcription source: [Habr article by illi4](https://habr.com/ru/articles/125484/)
- Program: [cooperative-cafe.txt](cooperative-cafe.txt)

## Description

A simulation where you run a small cooperative canteen.

## Gameplay

1. Start with `В/О С/П`.
2. In each day cycle allocate funds to:
   - food procurement (`E` phase),
   - advertising (`G` phase),
   - service quality (`C` phase).
3. The program tracks customers, empty seats, unsold food and reputation impact.

## Objective

Keep the cafe solvent and growing; avoid bankruptcy while balancing marketing, operations, and food waste.
